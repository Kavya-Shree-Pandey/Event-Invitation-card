Our Story

She bumped into him at the bookstore-literally.Her coffee almost spilled,his papers flew everywhere.They both laughed while picking them up, hands brushing.From that single touch, the world slowed down.

What started as an accidental meeting turned into late-night talks, shared secrets, and endless laughter. He marked her favorite lines in books; she brought his favorite coffee every morning.

Neither believed in "love at first sight," until they found themselves living it.

And just like that - two strangers became each other's forever.
